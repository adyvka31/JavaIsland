Gudeg

Gudeg is a typical dish from the Special Region of Yogyakarta which is made from young jackfruit cooked with coconut milk.

Soto Kudus

Soto Kudus is a soto originating from Kudus, Central Java. Soto Kudus contains shredded chicken and bean sprouts.

Rawon

Rawon is a typical Indonesian dish originating from Ponorogo, East Java, which is a black meat soup with a mixture of special spices.

Lontong Sayur

Lontong sayur or lontong gulai is an Indonesian food consisting of sliced ​​lontong served with vegetables cooked in thin coconut milk sauce.
