Lontong sayur or lontong gulai is an Indonesian food consisting of sliced ​​lontong served with vegetables cooked in thin coconut milk sauce. These vegetables are sliced ​​and mixed into the coconut milk sauce. The vegetables used include chayote, string beans, young jackfruit, then can be added with accompaniments such as tempeh, tofu, boiled eggs, chili sauce and crackers.[1] This dish can be found in Betawi,[2] and Minang traditions.[3]

Lontong sayur is very similar to ketupat sayur, the difference is only in the choice of using ketupat or lontong. In Jakarta this dish is a popular breakfast menu choice besides chicken porridge and uduk rice.[4]

Lontong gulai Minang memiliki bumbu kental khas masakan Minang. Kuah santan yang kaya bumbu rempah sudah dikenal sebagai salah satu cara memasak ala masakan Minang.[3] Beberapa lontong gulai ini antara lain; lontong gulai paku, lontong gulai tauco, lontong gulai cubadak. Makanan ini disajikan dengan telur bulat, mie goreng dan yang khas adalah lontong ini disajikan dengan kerupuk merah. Bahkan sekarang bersama lontong gulai juga di tambahkan tunjang. Lontong gulai biasanya dijadikan sarapan pagi bagi masyarakat Sumatera Barat.
